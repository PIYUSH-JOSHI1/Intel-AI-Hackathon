About RetailMind

Our mission is to transform retail through intelligent data insights

Our Story

RetailMind was founded in 2020 by a team of retail veterans and AI specialists who recognized a critical gap in the market: while retailers were collecting more data than ever before, they lacked the tools to transform that data into actionable intelligence.

What began as a small startup with a vision to democratize advanced analytics for retailers of all sizes has grown into a leading retail intelligence platform trusted by hundreds of retailers worldwide.

Our mission remains unchanged: to empower retailers with AI-powered insights that drive growth, efficiency, and exceptional customer experiences.
