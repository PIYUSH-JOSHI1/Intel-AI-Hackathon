Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our pricing and features

What's included in the free trial?

Our 14-day free trial includes full access to all features in the plan you select. No credit card is required to start, and you can cancel anytime during the trial period with no obligation.

Can I change plans later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll be billed the prorated difference immediately. When downgrading, changes will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

How do you count transactions?

A transaction is defined as any sale, refund, or exchange processed through your connected systems. For online retailers, this includes all orders. For physical stores, it includes all POS transactions.

Do you offer discounts for seasonal businesses?

Yes, we offer flexible seasonal plans for businesses with significant seasonal fluctuations. Contact our sales team to discuss options tailored to your specific business cycle.

What kind of support is included?

All plans include access to our knowledge base and community forum. Starter plans include email support with 24-hour response times. Professional plans add chat support with 4-hour response times. Enterprise plans include 24/7 phone, email, and chat support plus a dedicated account manager.

Is there a setup fee?

No, there are no setup fees for our standard plans. For Enterprise customers with complex integration needs, there may be a one-time implementation fee based on the scope of work required.
